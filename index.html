Dextromethorphan (DXM) is a Roblox Script created by Demo (now Moissanite), It is a universal script, with plans to include more games, currently specifically Project Delta by Viking Studios and Fallen Survival by Chunk Studios.


The Universal (which is the primary point of focus right now) contains an advanced Aimlock system, created to appear legitimate to people spectating/watching your gameplay, alongside a simple and easy to read Player ESP, and may contain miscellaneous features in the future.


DXM is most likely going to be an open sourced Project, That is the plan right now, but that might change in the future.
